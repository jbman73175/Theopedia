Benny Hinn
**Toufik Benedictus Hinn** is a Televangelist and proponent of the
[prosperity gospel](Prosperity_gospel "Prosperity gospel"), an idea
in line with the [Word of Faith](Word_of_Faith "Word of Faith")
movement.
