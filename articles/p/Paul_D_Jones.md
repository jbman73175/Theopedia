**Paul D. Jones** is a British theologian and Assistant Professor
of Western Religious Thought in the Department of Religious Studies
at the University of Virginia. He has a BA from the University of
Oxford and an MDiv and PhD from Harvard University where he studied
under Ronald Thiemann. Jones' dissertation, "The Humanity of
Christ: Christology in [Karl Barth’s](Karl_Barth "Karl Barth")
*Church Dogmatics*", was published with T&T Clark in 2008 and
[John Webster](John_Webster "John Webster") has noted that it "is
one of the very best studies of Barth to appear in recent years,
and one which promises to set the pace for future reflection on
Barth's understanding of the person of Christ." ^[[1]](#note-0)^
