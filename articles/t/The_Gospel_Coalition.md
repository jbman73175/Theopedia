2007 at a [TEDS](TEDS "TEDS") chapel where
[John Piper](John_Piper "John Piper"),
[Tim Keller](Tim_Keller "Tim Keller"), and
[D.A. Carson](D.A._Carson "D.A. Carson") spoke.
